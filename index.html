LLM connection

Plan A (default): free public open-source model via Pollinations.ai — no key, no signup, works out of the box.
Plan B: bring your own API key for a provider you prefer. Keys stay in your browser only.

⚠ Plan A calls a free public endpoint — expect occasional rate limits or slower responses. For Plan B, your key sits in your browser's localStorage and is sent directly to the provider you chose. Anyone on this device can read it — use a throwaway key with a spend limit for public pages.
